Summer Enrichment Program

The AwtyBots Robotics Enrichment Program provides students the opportunity to engage in a more STEM-focused environment, testing and challenging their capabilities as well as garnering their interest in the fields of technology and engineering. This program will not just teach the fundamentals of robotics and programming, but also problem-solving, critical thinking, and teambuilding skills.â€‹

Every summer the AwtyBots team will have a week-long summer camp at a select partnering school in which students will learn the basics of a robot and how it functions. Each day will build on the day prior and will test the participant's capabilities to adapt to multiple circumstances.

Curriculum

  • Day 1: Basic Maneuvers - Introduction to gears and sensors.
  • Day 2: Lifting + Carrying objects - Precise angles.
  • Day 3: Using sensors to follow a colored line + navigate a maze.
  • Day 4: LEGO FIRST practice challenge - Students will build a robot to accomplish a variety of tasks on a LEGO FIRST demo (map/field).
  • Day 5: Battle-Bots - Tournament for students to experiment with more freedom, utilizng multiple techniques.

As the program is close to half a day, breakfast and lunch will be provided to all participants. The program is also led by the Awtybots members and will be completely Awtybot-student-led.
